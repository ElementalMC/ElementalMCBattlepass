## Tracks the following stuff:

### Blocks
- Which blocks a player broke
- How many blocks a player broke
- Which blocks a player placed
- How many blocks a player placed
- Player interactions with blocks

### Regional 
- Which biomes were entered
- Player travel distance
- Which regions were entered

### Combat
- Amount of kills on mobs
- Amount of kills on specific mobs
- Timed amount of kills on mobs
- Amount of kills on mobs without dying
- Amount of player kills
- Player kill streaks
- Damage dealt
- Bounties placed
- Bounties claimed

### Bending
- Entities hit with bending
- Amount of times bending abilities were used
- Damage dealt using bending
- Healing done using bending
- Element specific actions

### Chat & Social
- Amount of messages sent
- Participation in chat games
- Amount of chat games won

### Economy
- Money earned
- Money spent
- Amount of items listed on auctionhouse
- Amount of items purchased off of auctionhouse
- Amount of items sold on the auctionhouse

### Crates
- Amount of crates opened
- Specific crates opened
- Crate rewards claimed
